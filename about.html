About Us

Established in 1993. This barber shop has been around for over 20 years and was recently bought By Danny. Danny wanted to offer a welcoming place where you could come and go as a friend. Danny remodeled the entire shop when he purchased it so he could offer the nicest and cleanest experience possible.

Meet the owner

Danny brings over 35 years of experience in the industry to the table. He started out at the age of 14 in his home country of El Salvador working in a barber shop. In 1981 he came to the states and continued to work in barber shops gaining a lot of experience and training from other highly qualified barbers.
